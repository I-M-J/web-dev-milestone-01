What I ate

You cannot visit Tokyo without trying the local food. Here are the ones I tasted:

  1. Fresh Sushi from the fish market.
  2. Tempura at a local restaurant.
  3. Hot Ramen from a vending machine shop.
  4. Savory Yakitori skewers in Shinjuku.
  5. Interactive Monjayaki in Tsukishima.
  6. A crispy Katsu Sando from the station.
  7. Sweet Taiyaki shaped like a fish!
  8. A refreshing Matcha Parfait in Harajuku.
  9. Wagyu Beef from a street stall.
My Favorite Foods
  • Wagyu Beef: It was so tender it felt like it melted in my mouth!
  • Yakitori in Memory Lane: Grilled chicken skewers in a tiny, smoky alleyway.
  • Matcha Parfait: A beautiful layered dessert I found in Harajuku.
  • Taiyaki: A warm, fish-shaped pastry filled with sweet custard.
